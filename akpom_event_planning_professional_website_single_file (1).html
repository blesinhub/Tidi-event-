Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

Do you travel outside your city?

Yes, we plan events nationwide in Nigeria. Travel fees apply based on distance.

Can you work with my budget?

Absolutely. We offer budget-friendly and premium options. We’ll advise where to save or splurge.

Do you handle vendors?

Yes — we source and manage trusted caterers, photographers, MCs, DJs, decorators, etc.

How early should I book?

For weddings, 3–6 months ahead is ideal. For birthdays/corporate, 2–8 weeks works.
